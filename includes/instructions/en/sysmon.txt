<h1>System Monitoring (SysMon) Task</h1>
<p>You oversee a set of six gauges. Correct the system when needed:
- when the green light turns off
- when the red light turns on
- or when one of the moving arrows reaches the end of any of the four scales.
</p>

<p>Controls:</p>
<p>Select a F1-F4 button to correct the specific pump with the same button name.</p>
