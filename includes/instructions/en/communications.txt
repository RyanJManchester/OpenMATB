<h1>Communications Task</h1>
<p>In the communications task, you respond to spoken instructions about radio frequencies.
Some messages are addressed to you (your own call sign displayed); messages addressed to a different call sign must be ignored.
</p>

<p>Controls:</p>
<p>Press the up and down keys to select the channel</p>
<p>Press the left and right keys to change the radio frequency to the requested number.</p>
