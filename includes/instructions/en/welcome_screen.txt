<h1>OpenMATB Overview</h1>
<p>OpenMATB is a multitask simulation environment first developed in 1992 by Comstock and Arnegard.
Because it requires participants to control several tasks at the same time, it enables a detailed
understanding of the mental processes involved in managing complex, dynamic situations.
</p>
